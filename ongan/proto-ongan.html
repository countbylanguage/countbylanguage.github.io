Ongan

Proto-Ongan is the reconstructed ancestor of the Ongan language family. It has only two numerals that are reconstructable, 1 and 2.

Here are the numbers one and two, translated to Proto-Ongan:

Number IPA
1 /waya/
2 /naga/

Source: "A Long Lost Sister of Proto-Austronesian? Proto-Ongan, Mother of Jarawa and Onge of the Andaman Islands" by Juliette Blevins
