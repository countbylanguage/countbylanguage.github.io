Bugun

Kho-Bwa > Bugun

Bugun is a Kho-Bwa language spoken in the Indian state of Arunachal Pradesh. It has about 1,000 speakers, and it may be an isolate.

Here are the numbers from zero to ten translated in Bugun:

Number IPA
0 //
1 /dzɛæ/
2 /nĩ/
3 /gʊʔm/
4 /βij/
5 /guwɛ/
6 /ɽɑk̚/
7 /mɪlej/
8 /nɛlʲɑʊ/
9 /tekej/
10 /sowɑ/

Source: "Bugun Talking Dictionary" by Gregory D. S. Anderson and K. David Harrison
