Pitjantjatjara

Pama–Nyungan > Wati > Western Desert > Pitjantjatjara

Pitjantjatjara is a Pama-Nyungan language spoken in Australia by about 3,000 people. It only has numerals for the numbers 1-3.

Here are the numbers from one to three, as well as the word "many", translated in Pitjantjatjara:

Number Romanisation IPA
1 kutju /kʊcʊ/
2 kutjara /kʊcɐrɐ/
3 maṉkurpa /mɐɳkʊrpɐ/
Many tjuṯa /cʊʈɐ/
