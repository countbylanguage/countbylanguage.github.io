Gbari

Niger-Congo? > Atlantic-Congo > Volta-Niger > NOI > Nupe-Gbagyi > Gbari

Gbari is an Atlantic-Congo language mostly spoken in Nigeria. It has over a million speakers, and it is closely related to the Gbagyi language. It is written with an unstandardised Latin script.

Here are the numbers from zero to ten translated in Gbari:

Number Romanisation IPA
0 //
1 gbmari /ɡ͡bmaɹi/
2 mba /mba/
3 nca /ɲca/
4 nnyi /ɲːi/
5 ntnu, gnuaatnu /ntnu/, /ŋʷaːtnu/
6 tnunwi /tnunʷi/
7 tnaba /tnaba/
8 tnaca /tnaca/
9 tnanyi /tnaɲi/
10 nyaawo /ɲaːwo/

Source: "A Dictionary of the Gbari Language" by Roger Blench and Musa Doma
