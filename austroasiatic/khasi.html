Khasi is an Austroasiatic language spoken mostly in India. It has around 1 million speakers and doesn't have a native script, so uses the Latin script.

Here are the numbers from zero to ten translated in Khasi:

Number Romanisation IPA
0 nod /noːt/
1 wei, uwei, iwei /wej/, /ʔuwej/, /ʔiwej/
2 ár /ʔaːr/
3 lái /laːj/
4 sáw /saːw/
5 san /san/
6 hynriew /hnriew/
7 hynñiew /hnɲiew/
8 phrá /pʰraː/
9 khyndái /kʰndaːj/
