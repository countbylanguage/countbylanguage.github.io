Mundari

Austroasiatic > Munda > North > Kherwarian > Mundaric > Mundari

Mundari is an Austroasiatic language spoken mainly in the Indian states of Jharkhand, Odisha, and West Bengal. It uses the Devanagari, Bengali and Odia script, but has also got a script made in 1980.
