Pnar

Austroasiatic > Khasi-Palaungic > Khasic > Khasi-Pnar-Lyngngam > Pnar

Pnar is an Austroasiatic language spoken mostly in India and Bangladesh. It has around 400,000 speakers and doesn't have a native script.

Here are the numbers from zero to ten translated in Pnar:
