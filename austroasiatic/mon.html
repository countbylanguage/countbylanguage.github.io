Mon

Austroasiatic > Monic > Mon

Mon is an Austroasiatic language spoken in Thailand and Myanmar. It has around 1 million speakers and uses a Mon-Burmese script.

Here are the numbers zero to ten written in Mon:

0 ၀ သုည, သုန်ည /suɲɛ̤ː/
