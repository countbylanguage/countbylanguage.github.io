Austroasiatic > Munda > North > Kherwarian > Mundaric > Ho

Ho is an Austroasiatic language spoken mainly in East India, in the states of Odisha, Jharkhand, Bihar, West Bengal, Assam, and Chhattisgarh. It is written in many regional scripts, but the Warang Ciki script is mostly used.
