Santali

Austroasiatic > Munda > North > Kherwarian > Santal > Santali

Santali is an Austroasiatic language spoken mainly in East India, Bangladesh, Nepal and Bhutan. It has been written in the Devanagari, Bengali, and Odia scripts, but the Ol Chiki script, made in 1925 for Santali, is now popular.
