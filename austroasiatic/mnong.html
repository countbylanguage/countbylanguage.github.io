Mnong

Austroasiatic > Bahnaric > South Bahnaric > Sre-Mnong > Mnong

Mnong is an Austroasiatic language spoken in parts of Vietnam and Cambodia. It has about 100,000 speakers.

Here are the numbers zero to ten written in Mnong, specifically the R'Lăm dialect:

Number Vietnamese Alphabet IPA
0 //
1 ju, muê, ƀơn /ɟuː/, /mue/, /ɓəːn/
2 bar /baːr/
3 tlau /tlaw/
4 puan, puôn /puaːn/, /puon/
5 prăm /pram/
6 pro /prɔː/
7 poh /pɔh/
8 pham /phaːm/
9 sư̆n /sɨn/
10 măt /mat/
