Odia

Indo-European > Indo-Iranian > Indo-Aryan > Eastern > Odia

Odia is an Indo-European language spoken mainly in the Indian state of Odisha. It has over 35 million speakers, as well as its own script.

Here are the numbers zero to ten written in Odia:
