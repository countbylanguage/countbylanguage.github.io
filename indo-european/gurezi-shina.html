Gurezi Shina

Indo-European > Indo-Iranian > Indo-Aryan > Eastern Dardic > Shinaic > Shina > Gurezi Shina

Gurezi Shina is an Indo-European language spoken predominantly in Gilgit-Baltistan in Pakistan and Kashmir in India. it can be considered a dialect of Shina. It is usually not written, but it can be written with the Nastaliq script. It has pitch accent, with a high and low tone.
