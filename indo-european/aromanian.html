Aromanian

Indo-European > Italic > Latino-Faliscan > Romance > Eastern Romanice > Balkan Romance > Aromanian

Aromanian is an Indo-European language spoken in the Southern Balkans, predominantly in Greece, Romania, and Albania. Although similar to Romanian, It is a different language, which has more influence from Greek, compared to Romanian's stronger Slavic influences.
