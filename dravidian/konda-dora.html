Konda-Dora

Dravidian > South Central > Gondi-Kui > Konda-Kui > Konda-Dora

Konda-Dora, also simply known as Konda, is a Dravidian language spoken in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. Due to its close proximity to Telugu, all numbers except 1-4 are Telugu. It has had a script developed for it, but it is not used often.

Here are the numbers from zero to ten in Konda-Dora:

Number Romanisation IPA
0 sunna /sunːa/
1 unṟi /unri/
2 ruṇḍi /ɾuɳɖi/
3 mūnṟi /muːnɾi/
4 nalgi /nalgi/
5 aidu /aid̪u/
6 āru /aːɾu/
7 ēṛu /eːɽu/
8 enimidi /enimid̪i/
9 tommidi /t̪omːid̪i/
10 padi /pad̪i/

Source: "Koṇḍa or Kūbi: A Dravidian Language" by Bhadriraju Krishnamurthi (page 247)
