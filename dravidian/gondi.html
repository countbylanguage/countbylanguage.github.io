Dravidian > South Central > Gondi-Kui > Gondi Languages > Gondi

Gondi is a tribal Dravidian language spoken mainly in the Indian states of Chhatisgarh, Maharashtra and Madhya Pradesh. The language uaually uses the Telugu script, but another script, Masaram Gondi, was developed in the early 20th century. However, in the early 21st century, a historical script, named Gunjala Gondi, was discovered and deciphered.
