Kaikadi

Dravidian > Southern > Tamil-Kannada > Tamil-Kodagu > Tamil-Malayalam > Tamil Languages > Yerukala-Korava-Kaikadi > Kaikadi

Kaikadi is a Dravidian language spoken in the Indian state of Maharashtra. It is spoken by a formerly nomadic community and has around 25,000 speakers.
