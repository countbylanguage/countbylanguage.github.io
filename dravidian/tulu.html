Tulu

Dravidian > South > Tulu Languages > Tulu

Tulu is a Dravidian language spoken in the Indian state of Karnataka. Despite having a relatively smaller number of speakers than other Dravidian languages, at 1.85 million native speakers, it has survived, partly due to its literary tradition. It uses the Kannada script now, but efforts are being made to revive the Tigalari script, which was originally used for it.
