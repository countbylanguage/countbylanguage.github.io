Adilabad Gondi

Dravidian > South Central > Gondi-Kui > Gondi Languages > Gondi > Adilabad Gondi

Adilabad Gondi is a dialect of Gondi, a tribal Dravidian language, spoken mainly in the Indian states of Maharashtra and Telangana. The language usually uses the Telugu script, but another script, Masaram Gondi, was developed in the early 20th century. However, in the early 21st century, a historical script, named Gunjala Gondi, was discovered and deciphered.
