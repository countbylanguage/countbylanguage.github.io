Brahui

Dravidian > North > Brahui

Brahui is a Dravidian language mostly spoken in Balochistan, Pakistan. Due to it being isolated from other Dravidian languages and being surrounded by Indo-Iranic languages, it has picked up a lot of vocabulary from Indo-Iranic, which can be seen in its numbers. Only numbers from one to three resemble other Dravidian languages.
