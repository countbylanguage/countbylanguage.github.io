Korra Koraga

Dravidian > Southern > Tulu Languages > Koraga > Korra Koraga

Korra Koraga is a dialect of Koraga, a Dravidian language spoken in the Indian states of Karnataka and Kerala, that is no longer mutually intelligible with another dialect, Mudu Korraga. Korra Koraga is influenced by Malayalam and uses either the Malayalam or Kannada script.
