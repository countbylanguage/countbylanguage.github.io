Kolami

Dravidian > Central > Kolami-Naiki > Kolami

Kolami is a Dravidian Language spoken in the Indian states of Telangana and Maharashtra, with about 100,000 speakers. It uses the Devanagari and Telugu scripts. It is similar to its cousin Naiki, but it has more Marathi influence.
