Kannada

Dravidian > South > Tamil-Kannada > Kannada–Badaga > Kannada

Kannada is a Dravidian language spoken mainly in the state of Karnataka. Its script may look more similar to Telugu, but it's more closely related to Tamil. Here are the numbers from zero to ten translated in Kannada:

4 nālku ೪ ನಾಲ್ಕು /naːlku/
