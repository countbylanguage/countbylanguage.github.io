Solega

Dravidian > Southern > Tamil–Kannada > Kannada > Solega

Solega is a Dravidian language spoken mainly in the Indian states of Karnataka and Tamil Nadu. It is very similar to Kannada, and uses both the Kannada script and a lot of its numbers.

Here are the numbers from zero to ten translated in Solega:
