Kodava

Dravidian > South > Tamil-Kannada > Tamil-Kodagu > Kodava

Kodava is a Dravidian language spoken mostly in the Indian state of Karnataka. It had an ancient script called the Thirke acript, but it eventually lost this and adopted the Kannada script. It has just over 100,000 speakers.
