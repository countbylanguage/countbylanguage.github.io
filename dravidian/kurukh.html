Kurukh

Dravidian > North > Kurukh-Malto > Kurukh

Kurukh is a Dravidian Language spoken in East India with about 2 million speakers, giving it more speakers than its cousin, Malto. Due to this, it has retained a few Dravidian numbers, even though it is surrounded by Indo-European languages. It has 2 new scripts created in the 1990's, however, neither are in Unicode yet.
