Alu Kurumba

Dravidian > South > Tamil-Kannada > Tamil-Kodagu > Kodagu > Alu Kurumba

Alu Kurumba is a Dravidian language spoken in the Nilgiri Hills, on the border of the Indian states of Tamil Nadu and Karnataka. It has about 2,400 speakers and is sometimes considered a dialect of Kannada.
