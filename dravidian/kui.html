Kui

Dravidian > South-Central > Gondi-Kui > Kuvi-Kui > Kui

Kui is a tribal Dravidian language spoken mainly in the Indian state of Odisha. Due to being situated in a predominantly Indo-European speaking state, it has adopted some Indo-European vocabulary, as well as the Odia script.
