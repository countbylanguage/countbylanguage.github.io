Kui

Dravidian > South-Central > Gondi-Kui > Kuvi-Kui > Kui

Kui is a tribal Dravidian language spoken mainly in the Indian state of Odisha. Due to being situated in a predominantly Odia speaking state, it has adopted some Odia vocabulary and the Odia script.
