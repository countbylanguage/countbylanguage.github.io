Pattapu

Dravidian > South > Tamil-Kannada > Tamil-Kodagu > Tamil-Malayalam > Tamil Languages > Yerukula-Korava-Kaikadi > Pattapu

Pattapu is a Dravidian language spoken in the Indian State of Andhra Pradesh. It has about 200,000 speakers and has been discovered recently.
