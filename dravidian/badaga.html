Badaga

Dravidian > South > Tamil-Kannada > Kannada–Badaga > Badaga

Badaga is a Dravidian language spoken mainly in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It is spoken by more than 100,000 people and uses the Tamil, Kannada, and Malayalam script, but a new script for the language specifically was created in 2009.
