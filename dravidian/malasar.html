Malasar

Dravidian > South > Tamil-Kannada > Tamil-Kodagu > Tamil-Malayalam > Tamil-Languages > Malasa-Eravallan > Malasar

Malasar is a tribal Dravidian language spoken in Southern India. It has a few thousand speakers and doesn't have a script.
