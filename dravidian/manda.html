Manda

South-Central > Gondi-Kui > Manda-Pengo > Manda

Manda is a Dravidian language spoken in the Indian state of Odisha. It is very small, having only 4,000 speakers, and is at risk of extinction due to all speakers being bilingual with the much larger Odia.
