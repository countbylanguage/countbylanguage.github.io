Proto-Austronesian

Austronesian

Proto-Austronesian is the ancestor of all Austronesian languages. No records of it survive, so Proto-Austronesian is reconstructed from the languages it has split into.

Here are the numbers from zero to ten translated in Proto-Austronesian:

1 asa, əsa /aça/
2 duSa /dusa/
3 telu /telu/
4 Səpat /səpat/
5 lima /lima/

8 walu /walu/
9 Siwa /siwa/
