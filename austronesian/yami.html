Yami

Austronesian > Malayo-Polynesian > Philippine > Batanic > Yami-Itbayat > Yami

Yami is an Austronesian language spoken in Taiwan by about 4000 people. It is the only member of the Malayo-Polynesian branch to be spoken in Taiwan.

Here are the numbers from zero to ten translated in Yami:
