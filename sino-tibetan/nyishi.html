Nyishi

Sino-Tibetan > Tani > Western Tani > Nyishi

Nyishi is a language spoken in the Indian state of Arunachal Pradesh. It has almost a million speakers and uses the Latin script.

Here are the numbers zero to ten written in Nyishi:

Number Romanisation IPA
0 //
1 aking /akiŋ/
2 anyi /aɲi/
3 om /om/
4 api /api/
5 ang, ango /aŋ/, /aŋo/
6 akye, akh /akie/, /ax/
7 ken, kan /ken/, /kan/
8 pin /piːn/
9 kiya /kija/
10 aryang /aɾjaŋ/
