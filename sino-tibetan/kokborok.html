Kokborok

Sino-Tibetan > Tibeto-Burman > Himalayish > Lepcha

Kokborok is a Sino-Tibetic language spoken in Northeast India, as well as parts of Nepal and Bhutan. It is an official language of the Indian state of Sikkim.
