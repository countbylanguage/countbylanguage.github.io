Sartang

Kho-Bwa > Mey-Sartang > Sartang

Sartang is a Kho-Bwa language spoken in the Indian state of Arunachal Pradesh. It has about 1,000 speakers, and is a primarily oral language. It can be written in the Devanagari script.
