Mangbutu

Nilo-Saharan? > Central Sudanic > Eastern > Mangbetu-Asoa > Mangbetu

Mangbutu is a Nilo-Saharan language spoken in the northeast of D.R. Congo. It has around 600,000 speakers, a rich phonemic inventory, and no script.
