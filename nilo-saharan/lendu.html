Lendu

Nilo-Saharan? > Central Sudanic > Eastern > Lenduic > Lendu

Lendu is a Nilo-Saharan language spoken in the northeast of D.R. Congo. It is one of the most populous Central Sudanic languages and has around 750,000 speakers. It has no script.
